HOW IT WORKS

Connected Oversight, Not Manual Collection

Safeheld connects your bank to every regulated firm in a hub-and-spoke model. Data flows automatically — no chasing, no spreadsheets, no lag.

Automated Data Collection

Firms using Safeheld automatically share safeguarding data with your bank. No manual requests, no email attachments, no delays.

Real-Time Status Feeds

Every reconciliation, every breach event, every resolution — streamed to your dashboard as it happens, not batched at end of day.

Regulatory Alignment

Reports and data structures built to meet FCA, PRA, and other regulatory expectations for safeguarding banks and credit institutions.

Segregated Access Controls

Bank users see only aggregate and summary data. Firm-level operational detail remains private unless explicitly shared.
