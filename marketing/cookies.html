Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website operators information about how their site is being used.

Similar technologies, such as local storage, session storage, and pixel tags, may also be used for the same purposes. When we refer to "cookies" in this policy, we include these similar technologies.

2. Cookies We Use

The Safeheld platform uses the following categories of cookies:

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to function and cannot be switched off. They are set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or setting your privacy preferences.

  • Authentication: Cookies that identify you as a logged-in user and maintain your session
  • Session management: Cookies that track your active session and ensure continuity as you navigate the platform
  • Security: Cookies that help protect your account and detect potential security threats, including CSRF tokens and MFA session identifiers

Essential cookies are always active and do not require your consent, as they are necessary for the platform to operate.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the platform, allowing us to identify areas for improvement and optimise performance.

  • Platform usage patterns: Cookies that record which features are used, how frequently, and in what order
  • Performance monitoring: Cookies that measure page load times, error rates, and other technical performance metrics

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised. It is not used to personally identify individual users.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences to provide a more personalised experience.

  • User interface preferences: Cookies that remember your chosen layout, sidebar state, and navigation preferences
  • Display settings: Cookies that store your preferred date formats, timezone, and table configurations

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View the cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or clear all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • Set preferences for first-party and third-party cookies separately

Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using certain features of the Safeheld platform. If you disable essential cookies, the platform may not function correctly.

For more information on managing cookies in your browser, consult your browser's help documentation or visit aboutcookies.org.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Safeheld uses minimal third-party services. We do not currently set any third-party advertising cookies on the platform. Where we use third-party tools for analytics or infrastructure purposes, we ensure that any cookies they set comply with applicable data protection laws and are limited to what is strictly necessary.

We will never allow third-party advertising cookies on the Safeheld platform. If our use of third-party cookies changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly and provide appropriate notice.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as our use of cookies evolves or as regulatory requirements change. Any material changes will be communicated via the platform. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was most recently revised.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy or how we use cookies, please contact us at privacy@safeheld.com.
