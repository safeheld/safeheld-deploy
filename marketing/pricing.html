FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?
We offer a guided demo and proof-of-concept period rather than an unassisted free trial. Because Safeheld integrates with your banking data and compliance workflows, our team walks you through the setup and ensures everything reconciles correctly before you go live.
What determines which plan I need?
Pricing is primarily based on the number of regulatory modules you need and the complexity of your entity structure. A single-licence EMI would typically use Essentials, while a firm managing both CASS and PS25 obligations would benefit from Professional.
Can I add modules later?
Absolutely. Safeheld is designed to be modular. You can start with a single regulatory module and add others as your obligations grow or as you expand into new jurisdictions. Upgrading between plans is seamless and does not require re-implementation.
How does bank dashboard access work?
The bank dashboard is included in the Enterprise plan. It gives safeguarding banks real-time oversight of the client funds held with them, including reconciliation status, breach history, and compliance summaries -- without exposing sensitive firm-level data.
What support is included?
All plans include onboarding assistance and email support. Professional adds priority response times and a dedicated Slack channel. Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager, custom SLAs, and quarterly compliance reviews.
Is my data secure?
Security is foundational to Safeheld. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive credentials, enforce multi-factor authentication, and maintain a full audit trail of every action taken on the platform.
