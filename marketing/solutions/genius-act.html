US REGULATION

GENIUS Act Compliance

US stablecoin regulation compliance under the GENIUS Act framework. Automated reserve composition verification, permitted asset monitoring, and monthly attestation support for stablecoin issuers operating in the United States.

Overview

The US Stablecoin Regulatory Framework

The GENIUS Act establishes a federal licensing framework for payment stablecoin issuers, with strict reserve requirements, monthly attestations, and redemption at par obligations. SafeHeld automates compliance.

Reserve Composition

Verify reserves are held exclusively in permitted assets: US dollars, Treasury bills (93 days or less), fully collateralised repos, and reserve balances at the Federal Reserve.

Monthly Attestation

Generate the monthly reserve attestation data required by the GENIUS Act, formatted for submission to registered public accounting firms for independent certification.

Issuer Dashboard

A unified compliance dashboard covering reserve adequacy, permitted asset limits, redemption readiness, and regulatory filing status for federal and state-level compliance.

Key Capabilities

Built for the GENIUS Act

Every capability maps to specific GENIUS Act provisions, ensuring issuers meet the full scope of their federal licensing obligations.

Section 4 - Reserves

Reserve Composition Verification

The GENIUS Act mandates that reserves consist only of permitted assets. SafeHeld continuously verifies that 100% of reserves are held in qualifying instruments, with no exposure to non-permitted asset classes.

  • US dollar cash and insured deposit verification at qualified custodians
  • US Treasury bill tracking with 93-day maturity limit enforcement
  • Repo agreement verification with qualifying collateral checks
  • Federal Reserve balance monitoring and reconciliation

Asset Monitoring

Permitted Asset Monitoring

Continuously monitor reserve portfolio composition to ensure all holdings remain within GENIUS Act permitted asset categories. Detect and alert on any drift toward non-qualifying instruments before it becomes a compliance breach.

  • Real-time asset classification against permitted categories
  • Treasury maturity countdown with rollover reminders
  • Counterparty exposure limits for deposit concentrations
  • FDIC insurance coverage verification for bank deposits

Attestation

Monthly Attestation Support

The GENIUS Act requires monthly attestation of reserves by a registered public accounting firm. SafeHeld prepares all attestation data, generates the supporting schedules, and provides auditor access portals for seamless verification.

  • Monthly reserve snapshot with full asset-level detail
  • Auditor access portal with read-only, time-limited credentials
  • Reconciliation between on-chain supply and reserve holdings
  • Historical attestation archive with tamper-proof record keeping

Dashboard

Issuer Compliance Dashboard

A unified compliance view covering every GENIUS Act obligation. Track reserve adequacy, permitted asset compliance, redemption readiness, and regulatory filing status from a single, real-time dashboard.

  • Real-time reserve ratio and backing status at a glance
  • Federal vs. state licensing status and renewal tracking
  • Redemption at par readiness verification and queue monitoring
  • Regulatory filing calendar with automated deadline reminders
