CRYPTO ASSETS

Crypto Asset Safeguarding and Proof of Reserves

On-chain wallet reconciliation, proof of reserves generation, and custody verification for crypto asset service providers.

Crypto asset service providers face unique safeguarding challenges. Client assets are held across hot wallets, cold storage, and third-party custodians. Traditional reconciliation methods cannot verify on-chain holdings.

Safeheld connects to blockchain data and custodian APIs to provide continuous, automated verification of client crypto asset holdings.

Capabilities

Comprehensive crypto safeguarding

Automated verification infrastructure purpose-built for digital asset custody and safeguarding compliance.

Wallet Reconciliation

Automated matching of internal client entitlement records against on-chain wallet balances across multiple blockchains and custodians.

Proof of Reserves

Cryptographically verifiable attestation that client assets are fully backed. SHA-256 hashed proof generation with complete data lineage.

Hot/Cold Wallet Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of wallet balances with configurable alerts for unexpected movements, threshold breaches, and anomalous patterns.

Data Lineage

Full provenance tracking for every data point used in reconciliation and attestation — from source through calculation to output.

Regulatory Alignment

Built for evolving regulation

Crypto asset custody requirements are evolving rapidly across jurisdictions. Safeheld's crypto module is designed to meet the safeguarding expectations of the UK FCA, EU MiCA regulation, and emerging US frameworks.
