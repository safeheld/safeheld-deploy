DIGITAL ASSETS

Crypto Custody Verification

On-chain wallet reconciliation, proof of reserves, and hot/cold wallet monitoring for crypto custodians, exchanges, and fund administrators. Verify holdings across every major blockchain.

Overview

Trust Through Cryptographic Verification

In digital assets, trust is not assumed -- it is verified. SafeHeld provides independent, on-chain verification that customer assets are held as claimed.

Multi-Chain Tracking

Monitor wallets across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Arbitrum, and other major L1 and L2 networks from a single dashboard with unified balance aggregation.

Proof of Reserves

Generate cryptographic proof of reserves using Merkle tree attestations, allowing independent verification that total on-chain holdings cover customer liabilities.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of hot and cold wallet balances with threshold-based alerts for unusual withdrawals, balance deviations, and unauthorised wallet movements.

Key Capabilities

On-Chain Verification Engine

Purpose-built infrastructure for verifying digital asset custody with cryptographic certainty.

Wallet Tracking

Multi-Chain Wallet Tracking

Register and monitor custody wallets across all major blockchains. SafeHeld queries on-chain state directly, bypassing API intermediaries to ensure data integrity.

  • Native support for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and 20+ networks
  • ERC-20, SPL, and other token standard balance tracking
  • Hot, cold, and warm wallet classification and tagging
  • DeFi protocol position tracking (staking, lending, LP)

Proof of Reserves

Cryptographic Reserve Proof Generation

Generate Merkle tree-based proof of reserves that allows anyone to verify your total holdings cover customer liabilities, without exposing individual customer data.

  • Merkle tree construction with hashed customer balances
  • On-chain address ownership verification via signed messages
  • Periodic attestation snapshots with tamper-proof timestamps
  • Individual customer verification portal for self-service checks

Reconciliation

Custody Reconciliation Engine

Reconcile on-chain wallet balances against internal ledger records and customer liability databases. Identify discrepancies between what you hold and what you owe before they become problems.

  • On-chain balance queries compared against internal ledger state
  • Pending transaction and mempool-aware balance calculations
  • Multi-asset reconciliation across native and token balances
  • Break investigation workflow with on-chain transaction tracing

Monitoring

Real-Time Balance Monitoring

Monitor hot and cold wallet balances continuously, with configurable alerts for balance thresholds, large withdrawals, and unusual transaction patterns that could indicate security incidents.

  • Hot wallet balance threshold monitoring with auto-alerts
  • Cold wallet access event detection and notification
  • Large withdrawal flagging with configurable thresholds
  • 24/7 monitoring with webhook and email alert channels

How It Works

From Wallet Registration to Verified Custody

Three steps to cryptographic proof that customer assets are safe.

1

Register Wallets

Add your custody wallet addresses and prove ownership via signed messages. SafeHeld begins monitoring on-chain balances across all registered chains.

2

Continuous Verification

SafeHeld queries on-chain state continuously, reconciles against your internal ledger, and monitors for threshold breaches, unusual activity, and balance deviations.

3

Publish Proofs

Generate Merkle tree proof of reserves, export compliance reports, and provide customer-facing verification portals -- all backed by on-chain data.
