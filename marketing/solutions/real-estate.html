REAL ESTATE

Client Deposit Protection

Protection of client deposits and funds held by property firms. Continuously verify that tenant deposits, buyer funds, and other client money held by estate agents and property managers are properly segregated and compliant with regulatory requirements.

Overview

Client Money in Property

Estate agents, lettings agents, and property managers handle significant client funds — from tenant deposits to exchange deposits. The Estate Agents Act 1979 and industry requirements demand proper segregation, insurance, and record-keeping.

Deposit Segregation

The Estate Agents Act requires client money to be held separately from business funds. Safeheld monitors account structures and balances to verify continuous segregation of all client deposits.

Deposit Protection Schemes

Tenancy deposits must be protected in a government-authorised scheme within 30 days. Safeheld tracks registration status and deadlines for every deposit your firm holds.

Redress & Audit

Property redress scheme membership (TPO or PRS) requires proper client money handling. Safeheld generates the evidence and audit trails needed for scheme compliance audits.

Key Capabilities

Built for Property Professionals

From high-street estate agents to large property management firms, Safeheld provides the continuous verification layer that proves client deposits are safe and compliant.

Reconciliation

Deposit Account Reconciliation

Reconcile client money accounts holding tenant deposits, exchange deposits, and rental income against your property management system records. Safeheld identifies discrepancies between the amounts you should be holding and the actual balances in your designated client accounts.

  • Match individual deposit records against bank account balances
  • Separate tracking of tenancy deposits, exchange deposits, and rent held
  • Shortfall alerts with severity grading and remediation timelines

Segregation

Client Money Segregation

The Estate Agents Act 1979 (section 14) requires estate agents to keep client money in a separate client account at an authorised institution. Safeheld monitors that your client account arrangements are properly maintained, with no commingling of client and business funds.

  • Monitor designated client account status with authorised banks
  • Detect potential commingling between client and office accounts
  • Track interest earned on client money and proper allocation
  • Alert on any withdrawal from client accounts that lacks matching authority

Compliance

Estate Agent Compliance

Meet your obligations under the Estate Agents Act 1979, the Consumer Rights Act 2015, and industry body requirements (ARLA Propertymark, NAEA Propertymark). Safeheld maps each regulatory requirement and monitors your firm's compliance status, from client money protection insurance to proper record-keeping practices.

  • Estate Agents Act 1979 section 14 client money compliance
  • Client Money Protection scheme membership verification
  • ARLA / NAEA Propertymark compliance audit support
  • National Trading Standards estate agent compliance readiness

Deposits

Deposit Protection Scheme Verification

Tenancy deposits must be registered with a government-authorised deposit protection scheme (DPS, TDS, or mydeposits) within 30 days of receipt. Safeheld tracks every deposit from receipt to registration, alerts you when deadlines approach, and verifies that prescribed information has been served to tenants.

  • 30-day registration deadline tracking for every tenancy deposit
  • Prescribed information service status per Housing Act 2004 s.213
  • Scheme registration verification across DPS, TDS, and mydeposits
