REAL ESTATE

Client Deposit Protection for Property Transactions

Automated verification of client deposit handling for estate agents and property transaction intermediaries.

Estate agents and property intermediaries handle significant client deposits during property transactions. These funds must be properly protected, segregated, and accounted for throughout the transaction lifecycle.

Safeheld automates deposit tracking, reconciliation, and compliance reporting for property transaction intermediaries — ensuring client deposits are properly protected at every stage.

Capabilities

What Safeheld Delivers

End-to-end deposit protection compliance automation for property intermediaries.

Deposit Reconciliation

Automated tracking and reconciliation of client deposits across multiple property transactions. Every deposit is matched from receipt through to completion or return.

Segregation Verification

Continuous verification that client deposits are held in designated client accounts separate from firm funds. Real-time monitoring of account balances against deposit obligations.

Transaction Lifecycle Tracking

End-to-end tracking of deposit handling from receipt through completion or return. Full visibility into the status of every client deposit at every stage of the transaction.

Compliance Reporting

Structured outputs for regulatory compliance and client assurance. Complete data lineage from source records through to final report figures with full audit trail.

Key Requirements

Deposit Protection Obligations

Safeheld addresses the core obligations that property intermediaries must meet for client deposit protection.

Deposit Protection

Client deposits safeguarded through proper segregation and protection arrangements throughout the transaction.

Account Segregation

Client deposits held in designated client accounts, fully separated from the firm's operational funds.

Transaction Records

Complete, accurate records of all deposit receipts, movements, and disbursements for every property transaction.

Timely Return

Prompt return of client deposits when transactions do not complete, with full tracking and evidence of return.

Interest Handling

Proper handling of interest earned on client deposits in accordance with regulatory and contractual requirements.

Dispute Resolution

Complete audit trail and evidence chain to support resolution of any disputes relating to client deposits.
