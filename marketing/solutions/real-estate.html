Real Estate

Client Deposit Protection for Property Transactions

Verification of client deposit handling for estate agents and property transaction intermediaries.

Estate agents and property intermediaries hold significant client deposits during property transactions. These funds must be properly protected, segregated from the firm's own money, and accounted for throughout the entire transaction lifecycle — from initial receipt through to completion or return.

Regulatory requirements and industry standards mandate that client deposits are held in designated client accounts, that accurate records are maintained for every transaction, and that funds are returned promptly when transactions do not complete. Firms must also manage interest obligations and maintain clear evidence chains to support dispute resolution.

Safeheld provides automated verification infrastructure that enables property intermediaries to demonstrate continuous compliance with deposit protection requirements — tracking every deposit from receipt through to final disbursement with full audit evidence.

Deposit Protection
