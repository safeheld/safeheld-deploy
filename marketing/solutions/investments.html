CASS Compliance Automation

Comprehensive CASS 6, CASS 7, CASS 10, and CMAR reporting for UK investment firms. Automate client money and custody asset obligations with continuous monitoring and audit-ready outputs.

Overview

The Full CASS Suite, Automated

Investment firms face complex client asset obligations spanning money, custody assets, and regulatory returns. SafeHeld automates all of it.

CASS 7 Client Money

Daily internal and external client money reconciliations, with automated acknowledgement letter tracking and compliant record-keeping for all designated client accounts.

CASS 6 Custody Assets

Automated custody asset reconciliation against third-party records, with holdings verification and registration checks for nominee accounts and sub-custodians.

CMAR & CASS 10

Automated CMAR return generation with pre-populated fields, plus CASS 10 resolution pack maintenance with real-time data for orderly wind-down scenarios.

Key Capabilities

Built for CASS Precision

Every feature maps directly to FCA CASS sourcebook requirements, delivering the rigour your compliance team and auditors expect.

CASS 7

Client Money Reconciliation

Perform both internal and external client money reconciliations as required under CASS 7.15. SafeHeld compares client money balances held at banks and custodians against your internal client money obligation.

  • Internal reconciliation: client ledger vs. client money requirement
  • External reconciliation: bank/custodian statements vs. firm records
  • Alternative and standard approach calculation support
  • Automated margin call and cleared trade adjustments

CASS 6

Custody Asset Verification

Verify custody assets held by nominees, sub-custodians, and third parties against your internal records. Track registration status and ensure assets are properly designated as client assets.

  • Multi-custodian holdings reconciliation with automated matching
  • Nominee and sub-custodian registration status checks
  • Physical and dematerialised asset tracking
  • Corporate action reconciliation and entitlement verification

CMAR

CMAR Report Generation

Automatically generate and validate your Client Money and Assets Return before submission to the FCA. SafeHeld pre-populates all fields from your live reconciliation data, reducing errors and manual effort.

  • Auto-populated CMAR fields from live reconciliation data
  • Pre-submission validation checks against FCA business rules
  • Historical CMAR comparison to detect reporting anomalies
  • Dual sign-off workflow for compliance officer approval

CASS 10

Resolution Pack Preparation

Maintain an up-to-date resolution pack that enables the orderly return of client assets in the event of firm failure. SafeHeld keeps all required data current and audit-ready at all times.

  • Real-time client entitlement calculations for all asset classes
  • Counterparty and custodian exposure mapping
  • Trust letter and acknowledgement tracking dashboard
  • Insolvency practitioner data pack export on demand

How It Works

End-to-End CASS Automation

From data feeds to regulatory submission in three steps.

1

Ingest Data Feeds

Connect bank accounts, custodian platforms, and internal ledger systems. SafeHeld normalises data from any source into a unified client money and asset model.
