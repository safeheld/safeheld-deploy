CASS Compliance for Investment Firms

Automated client money and custody asset reconciliation under FCA CASS 6, CASS 7, and CASS 10 rules, including CMAR report generation.

Overview

The CASS Compliance Challenge

Investment firms authorised by the FCA must comply with the Client Assets sourcebook (CASS). This includes daily client money reconciliation (CASS 7.6), custody asset reconciliation (CASS 6.6), and submission of the Client Money and Assets Return (CMAR).

Safeheld automates CASS compliance end-to-end — reconciliation, breach management, CMAR generation, and governance attestation.

Capabilities

What Safeheld Delivers

Full-spectrum CASS compliance automation for FCA-authorised investment firms.

CASS 7 Client Money

Automated internal and external client money reconciliation with configurable tolerance thresholds and break management. Daily verification that client money is held in designated accounts at approved banks.

CASS 6 Custody Assets

Reconciliation of custody assets between internal records and third-party custodians, with automated break detection. Continuous verification of asset positions and entitlements.

CMAR Generation

Automated generation of Client Money and Assets Return data, mapped to FCA submission requirements. Complete data lineage from source records through to CMAR figures.

CASS 10 Governance

Governance map, CASS oversight attestation workflows, resolution pack management, and compliance monitoring. Structured approval chains with full audit evidence.

Compliance

Key CASS Requirements Addressed

Safeheld maps directly to the obligations in the FCA Client Assets sourcebook.

Daily Reconciliation

Internal and external reconciliation performed on every business day as required by CASS 7.6.15. Automated matching with configurable thresholds and break tracking.

Adequate Safeguarding

Verification that client money is held in designated client accounts at approved banks. Continuous monitoring of segregation status across all banking relationships.

Breach Management

Detection, classification, and escalation of safeguarding breaches with regulatory notification workflows. Severity-based routing and resolution tracking.

Resolution Pack

Maintained and updated resolution pack information for orderly wind-down scenarios. Automated data collection ensures the pack is always current and complete.

Governance Oversight

CASS operational oversight framework with approval chains and attestation workflows. Structured governance mapping that satisfies CASS 10 requirements.

Audit Evidence

Complete audit trail for CASS audit, regulatory examination, and internal compliance review. Every action, calculation, and data point is immutably recorded.
