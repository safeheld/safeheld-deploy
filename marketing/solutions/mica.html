MiCA

Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation Compliance

Automated compliance infrastructure for CASPs, EMT issuers, and ART issuers under the EU Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation.

Overview

A New Era of EU Crypto Regulation

MiCA establishes a comprehensive regulatory framework for crypto-asset service providers across the European Union. It introduces specific requirements for client asset segregation, reserve backing for stablecoins, and ongoing prudential obligations.

Safeheld provides the verification infrastructure that CASPs and token issuers need to demonstrate continuous compliance with MiCA's safeguarding and reserve requirements.

Capabilities

What Safeheld Delivers

End-to-end MiCA compliance automation for crypto-asset service providers and token issuers.

CASP Asset Segregation

Automated verification that client crypto assets are properly segregated from proprietary holdings, as required by MiCA Article 70.

EMT Reserve Compliance

Continuous monitoring of e-money token reserves to ensure adequate backing with qualifying assets, meeting MiCA Title IV requirements.

ART Reserve Verification

Comprehensive reserve composition analysis for asset-referenced tokens, including diversification and liquidity requirements under MiCA Title III.

Regulatory Reporting

Structured outputs aligned to MiCA reporting obligations, including reserve adequacy attestations and prudential returns.

Regulation

Key MiCA Requirements

Core regulatory obligations that Safeheld addresses for CASPs and token issuers.

Asset Segregation (Art. 70)

Verification that client assets are held separately from CASP proprietary assets at all times.

Reserve Backing (Title III/IV)

Continuous proof that stablecoin reserves meet or exceed circulating supply with qualifying assets.

Prudential Requirements

Monitoring of own funds requirements and prudential safeguards for authorised CASPs.

Custody Obligations

Verification of custody arrangements and third-party custodian compliance.

Disclosure Requirements

Data infrastructure supporting MiCA's white paper and ongoing disclosure obligations.

Governance Controls

Compliance governance framework aligned to MiCA's organisational requirements for CASPs.
