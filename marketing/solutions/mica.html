EU REGULATION

MiCA Compliance Framework

EU Markets in Crypto-Assets regulation compliance for Crypto-Asset Service Providers and token issuers. Asset segregation verification, reserve monitoring, and regulatory reporting across EU member states.

Overview

MiCA Compliance, Automated

MiCA introduces comprehensive obligations for CASPs, ART issuers, and EMT issuers across the EU. SafeHeld automates the verification and reporting requirements so you can focus on growth.

CASP Obligations

Automated compliance with CASP prudential requirements, including client asset segregation, capital adequacy monitoring, and operational resilience controls under MiCA Title V.

ART & EMT Reserves

Verify reserve assets for Asset-Referenced Token and E-Money Token issuers, ensuring compliance with MiCA Title III and IV reserve composition and custody requirements.

Cross-Border Passporting

Track compliance status across EU member states for passported licences, with jurisdiction-specific reporting requirements and NCA notification support.

Key Capabilities

Built for MiCA Requirements

Every capability maps directly to specific MiCA articles, covering CASPs, ART issuers, and EMT issuers across the EU regulatory landscape.

Title V - CASPs

Asset Segregation Verification

CASPs must segregate client crypto-assets from their own holdings. SafeHeld verifies this segregation on-chain and provides continuous monitoring that client wallets are never commingled with proprietary positions.

  • On-chain verification of client vs. proprietary wallet segregation
  • Client entitlement register maintained in real time
  • Custody arrangement documentation and third-party custodian oversight
  • Commingling detection alerts with immediate escalation

Title III & IV

Reserve Requirements Monitoring

MiCA requires ART issuers to hold reserves in qualifying assets and EMT issuers to safeguard funds at credit institutions. SafeHeld monitors reserve composition, adequacy, and custody arrangements continuously.

  • ART reserve composition tracking against MiCA Article 36 requirements
  • EMT safeguarding verification at authorised credit institutions
  • Investment policy compliance monitoring for reserve portfolios
  • Significant ART thresholds monitoring (EUR 5B / 10M daily transactions)

Reporting

Regulatory Reporting for EU Authorities

Generate and submit regulatory reports to National Competent Authorities across EU member states. SafeHeld formats data to meet the specific requirements of each NCA and ESMA reporting templates.

  • ESMA-aligned regulatory reporting templates
  • NCA-specific formatting for BaFin, AMF, CySEC, and other authorities
  • White paper compliance tracking for token disclosures
  • Incident reporting and material change notification workflows

Passporting

Cross-Border Compliance

Manage compliance across multiple EU jurisdictions from a single platform. Track passporting status, jurisdiction-specific requirements, and maintain a unified compliance view for your MiCA obligations.

  • Passporting status tracker across EU member states
  • Jurisdiction-specific obligation mapping and deadline tracking
  • Unified compliance dashboard with per-jurisdiction drill-down
  • NCA notification workflow for service expansion and changes
