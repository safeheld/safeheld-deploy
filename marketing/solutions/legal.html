LEGAL

SRA Client Money Compliance

Automated compliance with Solicitors Regulation Authority client money rules. Continuously verify that client account balances are reconciled, interest is properly managed, and your firm meets every requirement of the SRA Accounts Rules.

Overview

Why Law Firms Need Automated Safeguarding

The SRA Accounts Rules 2019 require solicitors to keep client money separate, perform five-weekly reconciliations, and maintain accurate records at all times. Manual spreadsheets and quarterly checks leave gaps that put your practising certificate at risk.

Five-Weekly Reconciliation

SRA Rule 8.3 mandates reconciliation every five weeks. Safeheld automates this cycle so you never miss a deadline or file a late report.

Accounts Rules Compliance

Continuously validate adherence to SRA Accounts Rules 2019, including proper segregation of client and office money, and timely transfers between accounts.

Full Audit Trail

Every reconciliation, adjustment, and verification decision is logged with timestamps, producing SRA-ready documentation for inspections and compliance audits.

Key Capabilities

Built for SRA Accounts Rules

Every feature maps directly to specific SRA requirements, giving your COFA confidence that compliance obligations are being met continuously.

Reconciliation

Client Account Reconciliation

Automatically reconcile client account bank statements against your practice management system records. Safeheld identifies discrepancies between the client ledger, office ledger, and bank balances within minutes, not days.

  • Three-way reconciliation: client ledger vs office ledger vs bank statement
  • Automated five-weekly cycle aligned to SRA Rule 8.3 deadlines
  • Real-time mismatch alerts sent to the COFA and compliance team

Interest

Interest Management Verification

SRA Rule 7 requires firms to account to clients for a fair sum of interest earned on client money. Safeheld tracks interest accrual across all client accounts and flags cases where interest payments are overdue or below fair thresholds.

  • Monitor interest earned vs interest paid to clients on individual matters
  • Flag client accounts where interest has not been paid within policy thresholds
  • Produce interest calculation reports for client billing and SRA inspections

Compliance

SRA Accounts Rules Monitoring

Safeheld maps every relevant rule from the SRA Accounts Rules 2019 and monitors your firm's adherence in real time. From proper use of client accounts to timely transfers and the prohibition on using client money for firm purposes, every obligation is tracked.

  • Rule-by-rule compliance status dashboard for the COFA
  • Automated breach detection with severity classification and remediation guidance
  • Evidence collection for SRA Forensic Investigation Unit inspections
  • COFA-ready reports generated for annual compliance certification

Automation

Five-Weekly Reconciliation Automation

Stop relying on calendar reminders and manual spreadsheet comparisons. Safeheld automatically ingests bank data and practice management records on a five-weekly cycle, performs the reconciliation, and generates the required compliance report for the COFA to review and sign off.

  • Automatic data ingestion from major UK banking platforms
  • Integration with practice management systems (Clio, LEAP, Proclaim)
  • PDF reconciliation reports with digital sign-off workflow for COFA

How It Works

Three Steps to SRA Compliance

From data connection to COFA sign-off, Safeheld transforms your five-weekly reconciliation from a multi-day manual process into an automated workflow.

1

Connect Your Accounts

Link your client account bank feeds and practice management system. Safeheld supports all major UK banking platforms and legal PMS providers.

2

Automatic Reconciliation

Every five weeks, Safeheld performs the three-way reconciliation required by SRA Rule 8.3 and flags any discrepancies for review.

3

Review & Sign Off

Your COFA reviews the reconciliation report, resolves any flagged items, and digitally signs off. A complete audit trail is maintained for SRA inspections.
