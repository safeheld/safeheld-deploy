INSURANCE

Client Premium Safeguarding

FCA CASS 5 compliance for insurance brokers — continuous verification that client premium money is properly segregated, trust accounts are monitored, and statutory trust obligations are met at every stage of the premium handling chain.

Overview

Insurance Client Money Under CASS 5

Insurance intermediaries hold billions in client premiums under statutory or non-statutory trust. FCA CASS 5 requires rigorous segregation, daily reconciliation, and robust governance. Manual processes cannot keep pace.

Statutory Trust Compliance

CASS 5.3 requires insurance intermediaries to hold client money under statutory trust (or a non-statutory trust alternative). Safeheld continuously verifies trust status and account designations.

Daily Reconciliation

CASS 5.5.63R mandates daily calculation and reconciliation of client money balances. Safeheld automates this entirely, ensuring your internal records match bank statements every business day.

CMAR Reporting Support

Generate the data required for your Client Money and Assets Return submissions, with automated calculation of client money requirement figures aligned to FCA reporting periods.

Key Capabilities

Purpose-Built for Insurance Intermediaries

From Lloyd's brokers to regional intermediaries, Safeheld handles the specific complexities of insurance client money — IBA accounts, risk transfer, and the premium trust chain.

Reconciliation

Insurance Client Money Reconciliation

Perform the daily internal client money reconciliation required by CASS 5.5.63R. Safeheld compares your insurance broking system records against designated client money bank accounts, identifying discrepancies that could indicate a shortfall in client money held.

  • Daily internal client money calculation per CASS 5.5.63R
  • Separate tracking of IBA (Insurance Broking Account) and general client money
  • Automated shortfall detection with immediate escalation to CF10a

Monitoring

Trust Account Monitoring

CASS 5 requires client money to be held in properly designated trust accounts with approved banks. Safeheld monitors account designations, trust acknowledgement letters, and bank status to ensure your trust arrangements remain valid at all times.

  • Track trust acknowledgement letter status for every client money account
  • Monitor approved bank status against FCA approved institution lists
  • Alert when account designation changes or trust letters expire
  • Verify diversification of client money across multiple approved banks

Verification

Premium Handling Verification

Track the full lifecycle of client premiums from collection through to remittance to insurers. Safeheld verifies that premiums are handled correctly at every stage, with risk transfer points clearly identified and documented for regulatory purposes.

  • End-to-end premium flow tracking from policyholder to insurer
  • Risk transfer point identification per CASS 5.2 definitions
  • Aged premium analysis to flag delayed remittances to underwriters

Governance

Statutory Trust Compliance

Ensure your firm's client money arrangements meet CASS 5.3 statutory trust requirements. Safeheld monitors trust deed status, client money distribution documentation, and the governance framework required by your CF10a (CASS Operational Oversight function holder).

  • Statutory trust vs non-statutory trust arrangement tracking
  • CASS resolution pack documentation status monitoring
  • CF10a governance dashboard with escalation workflows
  • Annual CASS audit preparation with pre-populated evidence packs
