Client Premium Protection for Insurance Intermediaries

Client money verification for insurance brokers and intermediaries under FCA CASS 5.

Insurance intermediaries regulated by the FCA must comply with the Client Assets sourcebook (CASS 5), which governs the handling and protection of client money. CASS 5 requires firms to segregate client premiums from their own funds, perform regular reconciliations, and maintain accurate records of all client money movements.

Intermediaries must also manage Insurer Business Accounts (IBAs) appropriately, ensuring that funds held under risk transfer arrangements are properly categorised and tracked. The FCA expects firms to have robust systems and controls in place to prevent shortfalls and to detect breaches without delay.

Safeheld provides automated verification infrastructure that enables insurance intermediaries to demonstrate continuous compliance with CASS 5 requirements — from premium receipt through reconciliation to regulatory reporting.
