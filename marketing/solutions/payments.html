PS25 Safeguarding Compliance

Automated safeguarding for UK Electronic Money Institutions and Payment Institutions under FCA PS25 requirements. Replace manual spreadsheets with continuous, auditable verification of relevant funds.

Overview

Why PS25 Changes Everything for Safeguarding

The FCA's PS25 policy statement introduces prescriptive safeguarding requirements that demand daily reconciliation, real-time monitoring, and auditable controls across all relevant funds.

Daily Reconciliation

Automated daily comparison of relevant funds held against client liabilities, with full audit trail and exception reporting to meet the FCA's T+1 requirements.

Segregation Monitoring

Continuous verification that safeguarded funds remain properly segregated from operational accounts, with instant alerts on any commingling detected.

Regulatory Reporting

Pre-formatted reports aligned with FCA expectations, including safeguarding shortfall calculations, resolution pack data, and CASS-style regulatory returns.

Key Capabilities

Purpose-Built for PS25

Every capability maps directly to the FCA's prescriptive safeguarding requirements for EMIs and PIs.

Reconciliation

Daily Relevant Funds Reconciliation

Automatically ingest bank statements and transaction records to perform a daily comparison of relevant funds held in designated safeguarding accounts against calculated client liabilities.

  • Automated bank statement ingestion via Open Banking or CSV upload
  • T+1 reconciliation with full transaction-level matching
  • Unmatched items flagged and escalated automatically
  • Immutable audit trail for every reconciliation cycle

Segregation

Safeguarding Segregation Monitoring

Monitor that client funds remain in designated safeguarding accounts and are never commingled with the firm's own operational funds, as required by the FCA's segregation rules.

  • Real-time monitoring of designated safeguarding account balances
  • Automatic detection of non-client fund transfers
  • Account-level tagging and classification enforcement
  • Alert escalation to compliance officers and senior management

Detection

Safeguarding Shortfall Detection

Instantly identify when the total of relevant funds held falls below client liabilities. PS25 requires firms to top up shortfalls within strict timeframes -- SafeHeld tracks this automatically.

  • Real-time shortfall calculation against safeguarding requirement
  • Threshold-based alerts before shortfalls materialise
  • Automated top-up amount calculation with remediation timelines
  • Historical shortfall trend analysis and root cause tracking

Reporting

FCA Regulatory Reporting

Generate the regulatory returns and management information required by the FCA under PS25, including safeguarding reports, resolution pack data, and compliance attestations.

  • Pre-formatted FCA-aligned safeguarding reports
  • Resolution pack data maintained in real time
  • Board and senior management reporting packs
  • Exportable PDF and CSV formats for auditors

How It Works

From Data Ingestion to Compliance

SafeHeld automates the entire PS25 safeguarding workflow in three steps.

1

Connect Your Accounts

Link your safeguarding bank accounts and transaction systems via Open Banking APIs or secure file upload. SafeHeld ingests balances and movements daily.

2

Automated Reconciliation

SafeHeld performs T+1 reconciliation of relevant funds against client liabilities, flags discrepancies, and monitors segregation boundaries continuously.

3

Report & Remediate

Receive real-time alerts on shortfalls, generate FCA-aligned regulatory reports, and maintain a complete audit trail for supervisory review.
