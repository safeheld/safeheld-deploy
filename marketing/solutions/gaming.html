GAMING

Player Fund Protection

Gambling Commission player fund safeguarding compliance. Continuously verify that player balances are properly segregated, independently held in trust, and reported with full transparency to meet licence conditions.

Overview

Player Money Safeguarding Under LCCP

The Gambling Commission's Licence Conditions and Codes of Practice (LCCP) require all licensed operators to protect player funds. Operators must segregate funds and ensure players would be repaid in the event of insolvency.

Fund Segregation

LCCP condition 4.2.1 requires operators to segregate player funds from operational money. Safeheld monitors account structures and balances to verify separation is maintained at all times.

Independent Trust

The highest level of protection requires funds to be held in an independent trust or equivalent arrangement with an independent trustee. Safeheld verifies trust status and adequacy.

Transparency Reporting

Operators must publicly declare their level of player fund protection. Safeheld generates the evidence and reporting to support your declared protection level with confidence.

Key Capabilities

Designed for Gaming Operators

Whether you operate online casino, sports betting, bingo, or lottery products, Safeheld provides the verification layer that proves your player funds are safe.

Segregation

Player Fund Segregation Monitoring

Monitor the segregation of player funds from operational accounts in real time. Safeheld tracks balances across all designated player money accounts and alerts your compliance team the moment player fund balances fall below the total of customer liabilities.

  • Real-time comparison of player money held vs total customer liabilities
  • Multi-currency support for operators with international player bases
  • Automated alerts when segregation ratios approach warning thresholds

Trust

Independent Trust Verification

For operators seeking the highest level of player fund protection, Safeheld verifies that independent trust arrangements are properly constituted and that the independent trustee has adequate oversight of fund movements. This supports operators who declare the highest protection tier to the Gambling Commission.

  • Independent trustee status and appointment verification
  • Trust deed term monitoring and renewal alerting
  • Fund flow controls ensuring trustee is notified of material withdrawals
  • Evidence of insolvency protection suitable for Commission review

Reporting

Fund Adequacy Reporting

Generate comprehensive fund adequacy reports that demonstrate to the Gambling Commission that your player funds are sufficient to cover all customer liabilities. Safeheld produces daily snapshots and trend analysis, identifying any periods where coverage dipped below required thresholds.

  • Daily fund adequacy calculations with historical trend analysis
  • Exportable PDF reports for Gambling Commission licence reviews
  • Automated notifications to key management personnel on coverage changes

Licence

Licence Compliance Support

Your Gambling Commission licence conditions require specific player fund protection measures based on your declared protection level. Safeheld maps your obligations and provides a continuous compliance status, helping you avoid regulatory action and demonstrate good governance during licence reviews.

  • Map compliance status against LCCP conditions 4.2.1 through 4.2.3
  • Evidence packs for licence reviews and compliance assessments
  • Board-ready governance reports on player fund protection status
  • Proactive monitoring of regulatory updates affecting safeguarding requirements
