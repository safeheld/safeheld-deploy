GAMING

Player Fund Protection for Gaming Operators

Automated verification of player fund segregation for gambling operators licensed by the Gambling Commission.

The Gambling Commission requires operators to protect player funds through segregation, insurance, or trust arrangements. Operators must clearly disclose their level of player fund protection and maintain systems to prevent shortfalls.

Safeheld automates player fund reconciliation, monitoring, and reporting — ensuring gaming operators maintain continuous compliance with Gambling Commission requirements.

Capabilities

What Safeheld Delivers

End-to-end player fund compliance automation for gaming operators.

Player Fund Reconciliation

Automated matching of player balances against segregated bank accounts. Every player account balance is verified against the funds held in protection accounts.

Protection Level Monitoring

Continuous verification of player fund protection arrangements. The platform monitors that the declared level of protection is maintained at all times.

Shortfall Detection

Immediate identification of any shortfall between player balances and protected funds. Automatic severity classification and escalation ensure rapid resolution.

Regulatory Reporting

Structured outputs for Gambling Commission compliance and licence condition evidence. Complete data lineage from source records through to final report figures.

Key Requirements

Gambling Commission Obligations

Safeheld addresses the core obligations that gaming operators must meet under player fund protection rules.

Fund Segregation

Player funds held in separate, designated accounts protected from the operator's own business liabilities.

Player Balance Accuracy

Continuous verification that reported player balances match actual funds held in segregated protection accounts.

Protection Disclosure

Clear disclosure of the level of player fund protection in place, supported by verifiable evidence.

Event-Driven Monitoring

Real-time monitoring triggered by deposits, withdrawals, and balance changes to detect anomalies immediately.

Audit Trail

Complete, immutable record of every reconciliation, balance check, and compliance action for regulatory examination.

Licence Compliance

Evidence generation for Gambling Commission licence conditions relating to player fund protection.
