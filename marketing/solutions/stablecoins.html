STABLECOINS

Reserve Verification & Peg Monitoring

Independent verification of stablecoin reserves, peg stability monitoring, and issuer compliance. Prove that every token in circulation is backed by qualifying reserve assets.

Overview

The Trust Infrastructure for Stablecoins

Stablecoin holders need to know their tokens are backed. Regulators demand proof. SafeHeld provides the independent verification layer that bridges the gap.

Reserve Matching

Continuously verify that total reserve assets match or exceed the total supply of tokens in circulation, across all chains and issuance addresses.

Peg Monitoring

Track the stablecoin's market price against its target peg across major exchanges and DEXs, with alerts when deviations exceed configurable thresholds.

Issuer Compliance

Monitor reserve composition against regulatory requirements, track redemption capacity, and generate attestation reports for auditors and regulators.

Key Capabilities

Stablecoin Verification Engine

Purpose-built for the unique verification challenges of stablecoin reserves, peg stability, and redemption readiness.

Reserves

Reserve-to-Liability Matching

Reconcile the total supply of stablecoins on-chain against the reserve assets held by the issuer. SafeHeld verifies reserves across bank deposits, T-bills, money market funds, and other qualifying assets.

  • Multi-chain token supply aggregation across all issuance addresses
  • Reserve asset verification via bank feeds and custodian statements
  • Real-time reserve ratio calculation (reserves / circulating supply)
  • Reserve composition breakdown by asset class and maturity

Peg Stability

Peg Deviation Alerts

Monitor your stablecoin's price across centralised and decentralised exchanges in real time. Detect depegging events early with configurable alert thresholds and escalation chains.

  • Multi-venue price aggregation (CEX and DEX feeds)
  • Configurable deviation thresholds (e.g. 0.5%, 1%, 2%)
  • Volume-weighted price calculation to filter noise
  • Historical peg deviation analysis and trend reporting

Compliance

Issuer Compliance Reporting

Generate the attestation reports and compliance documentation required by regulators, auditors, and institutional counterparties. Track reserve composition against permitted asset requirements.

  • Monthly and quarterly attestation report generation
  • Reserve composition validation against permitted asset lists
  • Auditor access portal with read-only data export
  • Public transparency page for token holder confidence

Redemption

Redemption Capacity Verification

Verify that the issuer can honour redemption requests within required timeframes. SafeHeld tracks reserve liquidity, maturity profiles, and stress-tests redemption capacity under adverse scenarios.

  • Reserve liquidity assessment by asset maturity profile
  • Stress testing under 10%, 25%, and 50% redemption scenarios
  • Redemption queue monitoring and fulfilment tracking
  • Counterparty concentration risk analysis for reserve holdings

How It Works

Continuous Reserve Verification

From token supply tracking to public attestation in three steps.

1

Connect Reserve Data

Integrate reserve asset feeds from banks, custodians, and money market accounts. Register all token issuance and burn addresses across supported chains.

2

Continuous Matching

SafeHeld continuously reconciles total token supply against reserve assets, monitors peg stability across venues, and tests redemption capacity under stress scenarios.

3

Attest & Publish

Generate monthly attestation reports, provide auditor access portals, and publish a public transparency page showing reserve backing in real time.
