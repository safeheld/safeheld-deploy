About

Verification Infrastructure for Regulated Finance

Safeheld exists to solve a fundamental problem in financial regulation: proving that client money is where it should be.

For over eighteen years, the safeguarding of client funds has depended on manual reconciliation, spreadsheet-based controls, and periodic compliance checks. This approach is structurally unable to scale with the complexity of modern financial services — across multiple jurisdictions, asset classes, and regulatory frameworks.

Safeheld provides autonomous verification infrastructure purpose-built for the regulatory demands of payments, investments, crypto custody, and emerging digital finance frameworks. The platform operates continuously and independently, removing the operational burden of manual compliance processes.

Principles

Regulatory Precision

Every output is designed for regulatory examination. Reconciliation, breach detection, and reporting are built to the standards required by tier-one regulators.

Continuous Operation

The platform operates autonomously. No manual intervention, no gaps in coverage, no dependency on human reconciliation.

Institutional Standard

Infrastructure designed for the expectations of global banks, financial regulators, and compliance teams operating under FCA, EU, and US frameworks.

Regulatory Coverage

United Kingdom

FCA Client Assets sourcebook (CASS), Payment Services Regulations, e-money safeguarding requirements

European Union

Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation (MiCA), reserve and prudential requirements for CASPs and token issuers

United States

GENIUS Act stablecoin regulation, state-level money transmitter requirements

Global

Extensible verification framework adaptable to any jurisdiction and regulatory regime
