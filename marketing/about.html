ABOUT

Building Trust in Financial Infrastructure

Safeheld is the independent verification layer that proves client funds are safeguarded -- across every regulated sector and jurisdiction.

OUR MISSION

Proving Client Money Is Safe

Billions of pounds in client funds are held by regulated firms every day. Banks, payment institutions, investment platforms, and legal practices all hold money that does not belong to them -- and every one of those firms is required by law to safeguard it.

Yet the processes used to verify safeguarding compliance are still overwhelmingly manual. Spreadsheets, static reports, and periodic audits leave dangerous gaps between reality and what regulators see.

Safeheld provides the verification layer. We connect directly to the data -- bank balances, ledger records, on-chain wallets -- and reconcile it continuously. When something is wrong, we detect it immediately. When everything is right, we prove it.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Principles That Guide Us

Verification Over Trust

Compliance should be provable, not promissory. Every claim about the safety of client funds must be backed by data that can be independently verified in real time.

Automation Over Manual Work

Spreadsheet-based safeguarding cannot scale. The complexity of modern financial regulation demands automated, continuous verification -- not periodic manual checks.

Transparency Over Opacity

Banks, regulators, and auditors deserve real-time visibility. Safeheld makes compliance status clear, current, and accessible to every authorised stakeholder.

EXPERTISE
