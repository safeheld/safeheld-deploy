PLATFORM

The Autonomous Compliance Engine

Safeheld replaces the spreadsheets, the manual checks, and the guesswork. A single platform that reconciles, monitors, detects, and reports — continuously and autonomously — so your compliance team can focus on decisions, not data entry.

CORE ENGINE

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Three interconnected engines working together to keep your client funds provably safe — every day, without manual intervention.

Reconciliation Engine

Automated Matching, Zero Spreadsheets

Upload your ledger and bank data, and Safeheld automatically matches every transaction. Discrepancies are flagged instantly, with full audit context for every line.

  • Multi-source data ingestion (CSV, API, bank feeds)
  • Intelligent transaction matching with fuzzy tolerance
  • Daily automated reconciliation runs
  • Unmatched item tracking with resolution workflows

Breach Detection

Real-Time Monitoring, Instant Alerts

Safeheld continuously monitors fund balances against regulatory thresholds. The moment a shortfall or anomaly is detected, alerts fire to your compliance team and your safeguarding bank.

  • Continuous balance monitoring against obligations
  • Multi-channel alerts (email, dashboard, webhook)
  • Configurable breach thresholds and severity levels
  • Automated escalation and resolution tracking

Audit Trail

Immutable Logging, Complete Transparency

Every action, decision, and data change in Safeheld is recorded with an immutable, timestamped audit trail. When your regulator asks questions, the answers are already documented.

  • Tamper-proof event logging with hash chains
  • Full user action traceability (who, what, when)
  • Searchable audit log with advanced filtering
  • Exportable evidence packs for regulatory inquiries

ARCHITECTURE

Built for Scale and Security

A three-layer architecture designed for regulated financial services — from data ingestion to boardroom-ready reporting.

1

Data Ingestion

Ingest data from bank feeds, CSV uploads, APIs, and on-chain sources. Normalised and validated before processing.

2

Processing Engine

Automated reconciliation, breach detection, and compliance checks run continuously against your live data.

3

Reporting Layer

Audit-ready reports generated on demand or on schedule. PDF exports, dashboards, and regulatory submissions.

Bank-Grade Security

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. SOC 2 aligned controls with role-based access and MFA enforcement across every user.

Multi-Tenancy

Each firm operates in a logically isolated environment. Data never leaks across boundaries, and banks get aggregate views without accessing firm-level detail.

API-First

RESTful API with webhook support for every event. Integrate Safeheld into your existing tech stack or build custom workflows on top of the platform.

Real-Time Processing

Data changes trigger immediate recalculation. No batch jobs, no overnight delays. Your compliance status is always current.
