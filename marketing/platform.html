Core Capabilities

Reconciliation Engine

Automated matching of internal records against bank statements, custodian data, and on-chain records. Configurable tolerance thresholds, automated break resolution.

Breach Detection

Continuous monitoring for safeguarding breaches. Automatic severity classification, escalation workflows, and regulatory notification triggers.

Regulatory Reporting
