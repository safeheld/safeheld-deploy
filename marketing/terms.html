2. Description of Service

Safeheld provides automated client funds verification and compliance reporting for regulated firms. The platform enables electronic money institutions, payment institutions, investment firms, and other regulated entities to perform continuous reconciliation of client funds, detect safeguarding breaches, generate regulatory reports, maintain comprehensive audit trails, and manage governance processes related to client money protection.

The Service includes, but is not limited to, the following capabilities:

  • Automated daily reconciliation of client funds held across multiple safeguarding accounts and banking partners
  • Real-time breach detection and alerting when client funds fall below required safeguarding thresholds
  • Generation of regulatory compliance reports, including those required under the FCA's Payment Services Regulations and Client Assets Sourcebook (CASS)
  • Secure audit trail recording of all reconciliation activities, user actions, and system events
  • Bank dashboard functionality enabling safeguarding banks to oversee the funds held on their behalf
  • Governance workflows for managing safeguarding policies, approvals, and compliance sign-offs
