SECURITY

Security & Trust

Safeheld takes the protection of client data as seriously as the protection of client funds.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure

Safeheld is deployed on enterprise-grade European infrastructure hosted by Hetzner in Germany. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2+. Tenant environments are fully isolated, and redundant backups run automatically across geographically separated zones.

  • European data residency (Hetzner, Germany)
  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit)
  • Network isolation between tenant environments
  • Automated backups with geo-redundant storage

ACCESS CONTROLS

Defence in Depth

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions model with five distinct roles: Compliance Officer, Finance Ops, Auditor, Bank Viewer, and Admin. Each role sees only the data and actions relevant to their function.

Multi-Factor Authentication

TOTP-based multi-factor authentication on every account, enforced by policy. MFA secrets are AES-256-GCM encrypted in the database. No account can bypass the second factor.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action is logged with immutable, tamper-evident audit records. Full traceability of who did what, when, from where -- ready for regulatory inspection at any time.

DATA PROTECTION

Your Data, Protected

Safeheld is fully GDPR compliant with data residency in the EU and UK. We do not share data with third parties for any purpose beyond delivering the service. Our infrastructure undergoes regular penetration testing, and we provide data processing agreements to all customers.

  • GDPR and UK Data Protection Act 2018 compliant
  • Data residency in EU/UK jurisdictions
  • No third-party data sharing beyond service delivery
  • Regular penetration testing by independent assessors
  • Data processing agreements available for all customers
