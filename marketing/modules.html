Regulatory Modules

Sector-specific compliance, built on a shared engine

Each module is purpose-built for its regulatory regime while sharing Safeheld's core reconciliation, audit, and reporting infrastructure. Enable one or many — they work together seamlessly.

PS25 — Payment Services

Live

FCA safeguarding compliance for UK EMIs and payment institutions

Daily Reconciliation

Automated matching of relevant funds against segregated accounts. Internal and external reconciliation with variance reporting and full audit trail.

Breach Detection & FCA Reporting

Real-time shortfall monitoring with automatic severity classification. Breach acknowledgement workflows, remediation tracking, and FCA notification drafting.

Resolution Pack Management

Continuous health monitoring of all six resolution pack components. Automatic alerts when documents expire or acknowledgement letters need renewal.

Governance & Document Control

Centralised management of acknowledgement letters, safeguarding policies, due diligence records, insurance guarantees, and agent/distributor registers.

Board & Assurance Reporting

Automated generation of safeguarding returns, board packs, and assurance reports. PDF export with regulatory formatting.

Bank Dashboard

Aggregate oversight for banking partners across their entire EMI and PI client portfolio. Real-time compliance indicators per firm.

CASS — Client Assets Sourcebook

Live

CASS 6, 7, and 10 compliance for UK investment firms

Client Asset Custody Tracking

Full register of client assets held in custody, with asset type classification, valuation tracking, and status management across all custody accounts.

CMAR Submission Management

Quarterly and monthly Client Money and Assets Return preparation, validation, and submission tracking. Draft, review, submit, and archive workflows.

Risk Control Register

Categorised risk controls with likelihood/impact scoring, automated risk score calculation, and status tracking. Filter by category and review date.

Regulatory Update Monitoring

Track FCA regulatory changes relevant to CASS obligations. Impact assessment workflows with status management from identified through to implemented.

Impact Assessments

Link regulatory updates to impact assessments with action plans. Track assessment completion, affected areas, and required actions through to closure.

CASS Dashboard

Single-view overview of custody assets, CMAR status, high-risk controls, pending regulatory updates, and audit due dates.

Crypto — Digital Asset Custody

Live

Wallet reconciliation, proof of reserves, and data lineage for digital asset custodians

Wallet Management

Track hot, cold, warm, custodial, and omnibus wallets across Ethereum, Bitcoin, Solana, Polygon, and 5+ networks. Multisig support with signature tracking.

Balance Snapshots

Record wallet balances at any point in time, including block number references. Token-level tracking with USD valuation for cross-asset reporting.

Client Entitlement Tracking

Map client holdings against custodied assets at the token level. Per-client, per-token entitled balances with record date tracking.

Proof of Reserves

Generate cryptographic proof comparing total on-chain reserves against client entitlements. SHA-256 attestation hash, token-level breakdown, and reserve ratio computation.

Data Lineage

Full audit trail of every data transformation, import, validation, and attestation event. Source system tracking with parent-child event chains.

Crypto Dashboard

Real-time overview of total wallet count, active wallets, aggregated USD balance, unique tokens tracked, client count, and latest reserve ratio.

Stablecoin — Issuer Compliance

Live

Peg monitoring, reserve asset tracking, and reserve attestations for stablecoin issuers

Token Issuance Tracking

Register and monitor stablecoin tokens with peg currency, target price, circulating supply, and regulatory regime classification (MiCA, GENIUS Act, UK FCA).

Peg Monitoring

Automated deviation detection with four-tier classification: on-peg, minor deviation, major deviation, and depegged. Price, market cap, and volume tracking per snapshot.

Reserve Asset Management

Track reserve composition across 8 asset types: cash, treasury bills, government bonds, money market funds, commercial paper, CDs, crypto collateral, and more.

Reserve Attestations

Generate cryptographic attestations comparing total reserve value against circulating supply value. Coverage ratio computation with SHA-256 hashed proof for auditors.

Regulatory Regime Support

Built-in awareness of MiCA, GENIUS Act, UK FCA, and other regulatory frameworks. Module adapts attestation and reporting requirements per jurisdiction.

Stablecoin Dashboard

Overview of tracked tokens, on-peg status, total reserve value, coverage ratio, latest attestation date, and peg snapshot history.

Roadmap

More sectors, more jurisdictions

Safeheld's modular architecture is designed to expand across every regulated sector that holds client funds. Each new module plugs into the same core engine.

MiCA (EU)

EU Markets in Crypto-Assets regulation. Full compliance framework for crypto-asset service providers operating in the European Union.

GENIUS Act (US)

US stablecoin legislation. Reserve requirements, redemption rights, and operational standards for US-regulated stablecoin issuers.

SRA Client Money

Solicitors Regulation Authority rules for holding client money. Trust account reconciliation and reporting for UK law firms.

Insurance Premiums

FCA client premium rules for insurance intermediaries. Trust account segregation and reconciliation.

Gaming Player Funds

Gambling Commission requirements for protecting player funds held by licensed operators.

Real Estate Deposits

Client deposit protection rules for estate agents and property management companies.
